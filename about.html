We Are Aledoy

We are a digital agency that works with businesses looking to accelerate their digital transformation, to help them create real step change.

Aledoy Solutions Limited is a leading digital transformations company in Nigeria. Aledoy was registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) in the year 2014.

From inception, the core of our operations at Aledoy revolved around delivering websites, web application and business process solutions to our clients but having attained a notable landmark and setting a standard ahead of our contemporaries, the leadership of our organization deemed it worthy to expand our horizon. This led to an expansion of the services we render, as we delved into the world of absolute digital transformations.
